About Life In A Box

Life in a Box was created from the combined passion and expertise of three stores: Chase Aquascape, Chase Pets, and Slice of Nature. These stores are owned by a team that shares a love for aquatic life and pet care. By bringing them together, we built an all in one e-commerce platform that offers high quality aquariums, fish, pet supplies, and custom aquarium design services. Our goal is to make it easier for hobbyists and enthusiasts to find everything they need in one place while ensuring top notch quality and customer service.
