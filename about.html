Who are we

At Chase Aquatics, our mission is to bring a slice of nature into your home through beautifully crafted aquascapes. We design serene, natural aquatic environments that enhance your living space, promote relaxation, and foster a deeper connection to nature. Our goal is to make nature more accessible by blending aquatic plants, fish, and hardscape elements to create a harmonious, calming atmosphere indoors.

Joseph Flores, founder and lead aquascape artist of Chase Aquatics, is a passionate nature enthusiast and former Regional Franchise Manager. His journey into aquascaping began as a personal hobby and quickly evolved into a full-time pursuit. With experience facilitating national aquascaping competitions, Joseph is dedicated to inspiring others and helping artists grow in this unique and therapeutic art form.
